About Freya

I am a Magyar Cica, which means I am a Hungarian Cat. I was born in Kecskemet on an unknown date. My mom was a sweet stray white cat with beautiful eyes, my father was a grumpy orange cat.

I had three siblings. My sister and I were adopted on September 16th 2018, which I took as my birthday.

My brother was adopted by someone else in Hungary.

I was given the name Freya and my sister was named Flame. We moved to Mako with some humans.
